About AeroLink Uganda

Uganda's premier domestic safari airline

Since 2012, AeroLink Uganda has been connecting travellers to Uganda's most breathtaking national parks. Operating from Entebbe International Airport, we make safari dreams accessible in just 75 minutes.

A subsidiary of AirKenya Express, we operate a fleet of Cessna Grand Caravans flown by two experienced pilots, offering panoramic views through clear glass cockpits.
